About Us

Our company understands that street lighting is a core piece of urban and rural infrastructure. We design and manufacture with maximum supervision and our team continously strives to deliver quality products to our customers. Our solutions for transmission poles and substation structures are custom-engineered, fabricated and delivered to meet the expectations of our client, and perhaps that's what sets us apart as a pioneer in the industry. Our companny spares no effort to supply cost effective, aesthetically pleasing and resilient products, customer satisfaction being our priority.
